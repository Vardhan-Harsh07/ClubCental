About Us

Welcome to the Bennett University Western Dance Club! We are a group of passionate dancers who love all things country-western. Our club is dedicated to spreading our love of dance to the college community and beyond. At our club, we focus on various styles of country-western dance, including line dancing, two-step, and swing. We welcome dancers of all levels, from beginners to experienced dancers. Our goal is to create a fun and welcoming environment where everyone can learn, grow, and have a great time. Our club hosts regular dance events, workshops, and performances throughout the year. These events are a great way to showcase our skills, connect with other dancers, and build community. We also participate in various competitions and festivals, both on and off campus. Our club is more than just a place to dance - it's a community. We are a group of diverse individuals who share a passion for country-western dance. Whether you're looking to learn a new skill, make new friends, or just have fun, the Bennett University Western Dance Club is the perfect place for you. So come on out and join us! We can't wait to meet you and share our love of dance with you.
