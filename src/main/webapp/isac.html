ISAC

ISAC is the media club of Bennett University. It is the place for aspiring photographers, filmmakers, designers and editors to pool and showcase their skills.

About Us

At ISAC Photography Club, we provide a supportive and creative environment for members to explore and develop their photography skills. We offer workshops, photo walks, and other events that provide opportunities to learn new techniques, share feedback and collaborate with other members. Our club is a space where international students can connect with one another through their shared love of photography. We encourage members to share their cultural backgrounds and experiences, using photography as a medium to express their unique perspectives and narratives. Our club is open to all levels of photography experience and equipment, from smartphones to DSLRs. We welcome anyone who shares our passion for photography and wants to learn, create, and explore with a supportive community. Overall, ISAC Photography Club is a fun and engaging way for international students at our university to connect with others who share their love of photography and explore their creative potential. We look forward to welcoming you to our community!
